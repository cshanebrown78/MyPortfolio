Brief Background


Hello, my name is Christopher Shane Brown. I know you are wondering if I go by Christopher or Chris, but I actually go by Shane. In 1996 I graduated from Athens High School and started studying at the University of Texas where I graduated with a bachelors degree in Economics and a business foundations certificate. After spending more than 10 years as a Financial Analyst and being laid off twice in 3 years I decided to make a change. Having always liked programming and computers in school, I decided that coding boot camp was a good path for me and started a program to become a full stack web developer. This was something that I could use as a career and could be combined with my Analyst experience
